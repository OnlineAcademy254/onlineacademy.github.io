About Us

Welcome to Fanaka Schools, a distinguished girls' high school located in the heart of Narok. We are a vibrant Christian- based school dedicated to nurturing young girls who are empowered, inspired and prepared to make a positive impact in the world. Our mission is to provide a holistic education grounded in christian value where academic excellence and spiritual grouth go hand in hand. We are commited to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment that encourage each student to discover her unique potential and pursue her dream with confidence. We have a strong reputation based on our four pillars;Resposibility, Creativity & innovation, Competencies and Christianity
